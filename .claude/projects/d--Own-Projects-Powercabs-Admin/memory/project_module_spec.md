All 17 admin panel modules and their features (from Module Specification PDF, 7 May 2026):

01 Dashboard — Live overview, revenue summary (today/week/month), KPIs & real-time alerts
02 Ride Management — All rides (live/scheduled/completed/cancelled), ride details, manual assignment, disputes
03 Dispatcher Console — Live map with driver locations, manual booking, assign/reassign drivers, call/chat
04 Driver Management — Profiles & docs (license/insurance/vehicle), approval/suspension workflow, online status & activity, earnings & payouts, ratings, background check status
05 Passenger Management — Profiles, ride history, account status (active/banned), wallet & payment methods, complaints & feedback
06 Corporate Accounts — Client profiles, employee/user management, ride policies (spend limits/zones), invoicing & billing, usage reports
07 Fleet Management — Vehicle registry (linked to drivers), vehicle types (Economy/XL), inspection & compliance, document expiry alerts
08 Finance & Payments — Revenue breakdown, driver payouts, refunds & adjustments, payment gateway transactions, commission settings, corporate invoices, CSV/PDF export
09 Promotions & Pricing — Surge pricing rules, discount codes & campaigns, referral program, zone-based fare rules
10 Zones & Coverage — Service area (map-based), zone creation/editing, restricted/no-go zones, airport/fixed-fare zones
11 Notifications & Alerts — Push notification campaigns, SMS & email broadcast, system alerts config, scheduled announcements
12 Analytics & Reports — Revenue analytics, ride volume trends, driver performance, passenger retention & churn, heatmaps, custom date range, CSV/PDF export
13 Support & Disputes — Helpdesk tickets, dispute resolution workflow, complaint categories & tagging, SLA tracking
14 Ratings & Reviews — Driver ratings overview, passenger ratings, flag/remove inappropriate reviews
15 Settings & Configuration — App version control, ride types & vehicle categories, commission & fee structure, surge multiplier, referral & promo rules, terms & privacy content, email/SMS template management
16 Admin User Management — Roles & permissions, audit logs, 2FA, session management
17 Integrations — Payment gateway (Stripe), TURN server/WebRTC, Supabase/API health, Google Maps API, SMS gateway

**How to apply:** When building any module, implement exactly these features. Use this as the source of truth for scope.
